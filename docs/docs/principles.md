# Core Principles

**Sentinel Security Protocol**

## Foundational Principles

### 1. True Digital Sovereignty
Users maintain full control over their data, computation, and identity. External parties cannot access, track, or extract information without authorization.

### 2. Physical & Technical Isolation
Strong physical and electromagnetic isolation (Faraday-grade) combined with architectural controls to prevent unauthorized access or leakage.

### 3. Autonomous Verification
Mathematical and autonomous verification mechanisms that reduce reliance on trusted third parties and increase system integrity.

### 4. Physical-Reality Anchoring
Systems are grounded in verifiable physical reality to resist purely digital manipulation or spoofing.

### 5. Local-First & Edge Processing
Wherever possible, processing and decision-making occur locally or at the edge to maximize privacy and minimize external dependencies.

### 6. Recursive & Transparent Governance
Governance and auditing systems are themselves auditable (“IA of IA”), creating continuous accountability.

### 7. Defense in Depth
No single point of failure. Multiple independent layers work together to protect sovereignty.

### 8. Non-Proprietary Transparency
High-level concepts, architecture, and strategic analysis are shared publicly. Proprietary implementations and specific technical details remain private.

## Goal

To architect systems in which the device becomes an **impenetrable sovereign vault** — reversing the dynamics of surveillance capitalism through deliberate technical design.
